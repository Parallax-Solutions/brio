# Vision

## Long-Term Goals

The Costa Rica Multi-Currency Budget App aims to become the go-to personal finance management tool for multi-currency households in Costa Rica and similar markets.

## Future Features

### Household Mode (V2+)
- **Multi-user support**: Allow multiple household members to contribute to the same budget
- **Shared income sources**: Track income from all household members
- **Role-based permissions**: Different access levels (admin, contributor, viewer)
- **Household member profiles**: Individual spending tracking within a shared budget

### Advanced Reporting & Analytics (V2+)
- **Spending trends**: Visual charts showing spending patterns over time
- **Category analysis**: Deep dives into spending by category with comparisons
- **Savings goals**: Set and track savings targets with progress visualization
- **Budget vs. actual**: Compare planned vs. actual spending
- **Export capabilities**: CSV/PDF exports for tax preparation and record-keeping
- **Annual summaries**: Year-end financial reports

### Enhanced Currency Features (V2+)
- **Live exchange rate integration**: Automatic fetching from financial APIs
- **Rate alerts**: Notifications when exchange rates change significantly
- **Multi-base currency support**: Allow users to view budgets in any currency
- **Currency hedging insights**: Suggestions for optimal currency conversion timing

### Mobile App (V3+)
- **Native iOS/Android apps**: Full-featured mobile experience
- **Offline support**: Work without internet connection
- **Receipt scanning**: OCR for automatic expense entry
- **Push notifications**: Alerts for upcoming payments and budget thresholds

### Integration & Automation (V3+)
- **Bank account integration**: Connect to Costa Rican and international banks
- **Automatic transaction import**: Sync transactions from connected accounts
- **Smart categorization**: AI-powered expense categorization
- **Recurring payment detection**: Automatically detect and suggest recurring payments

### Advanced Budgeting (V3+)

- **Rollover budgets**: Unspent amounts roll over to next month
- **Budget templates**: Pre-configured budget templates for different lifestyles

### Collaboration & Sharing (V3+)
- **Shared budgets with partners**: Collaborative budgeting for couples
- **Financial advisor access**: Grant read-only access to financial advisors
- **Family financial education**: Tools to teach children about budgeting

### Security & Privacy (Ongoing)
- **End-to-end encryption**: Protect sensitive financial data
- **Two-factor authentication**: Enhanced security for account access
- **Data export & deletion**: Full control over personal data
- **Privacy-first architecture**: Minimal data collection, maximum privacy

## Market Expansion

While initially focused on Costa Rica (CRC, USD, CAD), the app architecture supports expansion to:
- Other Central American countries
- Caribbean nations with multi-currency needs
- Any market where multi-currency budgeting is essential

- **User retention**: Monthly active users who consistently track their finances
- **Accuracy**: Zero calculation errors in currency conversions
- **Accessibility**: 100% WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- **Performance**: Sub-second page loads and instant budget calculations
- **User satisfaction**: High ratings for ease of use and financial clarity
